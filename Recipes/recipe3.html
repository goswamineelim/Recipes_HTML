Cheese Cake

Cheesecake is a dessert made with a soft fresh cheese (typically cottage cheese, cream cheese, quark or ricotta), eggs, and sugar.

Ingredients :
  • Sunflower Oil
  • Digestive Biscuits
  • Butter
  • Cinnamon
  • White Chocolate
  • Strawberries
  • Cream Cheese
  • Vanilla Extract
  • dried parsley
  • Lemon
  • Maple Syrup
  • Double Cream
Recipe :
  1. Make the biscuits
  2. Mix the rest of the ingredients.
  3. Layer the ingredients.
  4. Cover with foil and bake.
  5. Let the cheesecake rest before serving.
